Serenade Assisted Living

Serenade Assisted Living offers a uniquely peaceful setting where elevated dining meets wide-open Arizona scenery. Guests enjoy beautiful sightlines toward the San Tan Mountains, especially during golden hour and sunset.

One of the most memorable parts of this location is the surrounding farm-life atmosphere. You’ll often see animals on nearby properties and around the local area, creating a warm and distinctive countryside feel that pairs perfectly with the Sky High experience.

  • Open mountain-facing views of the San Tan range
  • Farm-adjacent atmosphere with visible local animals
  • Quiet, scenic environment ideal for celebrations and special moments
